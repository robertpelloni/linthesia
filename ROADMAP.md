# Roadmap for Linthesia

This document outlines the planned features, improvements, and known gaps in Linthesia.

## Current Status (v1.4.0-dev)
Linthesia is a fully functional, feature-complete MIDI visualizer and learning tool for Linux.

### In Progress: v1.4.0
*   **Sheet Music Polish**: Measure lines and ledger lines added.
*   **Particle Effects**: Visual "juice" on key hits.

### Major Milestone: v1.3.0
*   **Sheet Music**: Basic staff rendering integrated.
*   **Scoring Polish**: Early/Late indicators.

## Future Horizons

### Post-v1.4 Goals
*   **Sheet Music**: Key Signatures, Beams, Clef symbols.
*   **Cross-Platform Port**: Windows/macOS backends.
*   **Built-in Synthesizer**: FluidSynth.
